Welcome to the Audiovista Project

An “Audiovista” is a sequence of 360° images accompanied by narration or other recorded audio.

When it’s viewed using a virtual reality system such as a Google Cardboard headset, the Audiovista can be a powerful way to transport a viewer to a different time and place. It’s an excellent platform for many educational purposes.

How to make your own!

Here is a video tutorial walking through the process of using iMovie to make an Audiovista for sharing on YouTube. You will need a camera that can capture 360° images (such as a Ricoh Theta or LG 360), and some way of recording audio (a smartphone works fine for this).
